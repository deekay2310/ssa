Stainless Steel Flat Bar

Shatrunjay Special Alloy Manufactures and Exports Stainless Steel rolled Flat Bars and flat bars in hot finished condition. The Stainless Steel Flat Bars that Shatrunjay Special Alloy manufacture, export and supply are known for their high strength. We manufacture our Stainless Steel Flat Bars using the best quality sturdy steel to guarantee corrosion resistance. Our Stainless Steel Flat Bars are not affected by early wear and tear. These Stainless Steel Flat Bars can be obtained at economic prices.

Stainless Steel is an austenitic chromium-nickel-manganese stainless steel which was developed to conserve nickel. Stainless Steelis a lower cost alternative to conventional Cr-Ni stainless steels such as 301 and 304. Nickel is replaced by additions of manganese and nitrogen. It is non-hardenable by thermal treatment, but may be cold worked to high tensile strengths. Stainless Steel is essentially nonmagnetic in the annealed condition and becomes magnetic when cold worked. We can also polish stainless rolled flat bars per your specifications. Other grades and non standard sizes can be supplied upon request. Please contact a sales associate for more information regarding our stainless inventory and processing capabilities.
