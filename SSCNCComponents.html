Stainless Steel CNC Components

Shatrunjay Special Alloy is an Indian Based Offshore manufacturer of precision machined Auto parts, aerospace and medical components and assemblies and has been Supplying and Exporting Precision CNC/VMC components in India and other countries such as UK, US, MIDDLE EAST, OMAN, KUWAIT, QUATAR, CZECH REPUBLIC, TURKEY and many other countries. Shatrunjay Special Alloy produces close-tolerance, precision machined auto parts, aerospace and medical components upto 300mm diameter. The process include Swiss screw machining, turning, milling, center-less grinding, assembly and supply-chain logistics. Shatrunjay Special Alloy Specialities include fasteners, pins, valve bodies, bushings, pistons, sleeves, housings, medical implants, medical plates, auto parts and other custom CNC and VMC precision machined components.
Shatrunjay Special Alloy Produces the Precision components in the following grades such as Stainless steel 202, 304/304L, 316/316L, 304H, 316H, 309/S, 310/S, 317/L, 321/H, 347/H, 904L, 330, 254SMO, 410, Duplex 2205 and 2507. Nickel Alloy such as alloy 200/201, C276, C22, Alloy 400, Alloy 600, Alloy 625, Alloy 800H, Alloy 800HT, 254SMO, AL6XN and Titanium Grade 1, Grade 2, Grade 5 and all other grades in Copper alloys .
Shatrunjay Special Alloy qualified personnel’s are highly trained engineers and are responsible for Manufacturing quality products as per customer requirements drawings and making it a reality. Being a master in supply and export of raw material Shatrunjay Special Alloyimports the raw material as well as fabricates it making the it cost reducing and raw materials in metals are imported from reputed mills . Shatrunjay Special Alloy Team follows every production job from start to finish, working closely with the requirements of the clients to meet his expectation and produce quality products.Shatrunjay Special Alloy diametrical tolerances of +/-.005 mm and position tolerances of .0254 mm for the precision products and parts manufactured.
