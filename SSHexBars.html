Stainless Steel is an austenitic chromium-nickel-manganese stainless steel which was developed to conserve nickel. Stainless Steel washers is a lower cost alternative to conventional Cr-Ni stainless steels such as 301 and 304. Nickel is replaced by additions of manganese and nitrogen. It is non-hardenable by thermal treatment, but may be cold worked to high tensile strengths. Stainless Steel washers is essentially nonmagnetic in the annealed condition and becomes magnetic when cold worked.

Stainless Steel Bar Value Added Options:
1] Stainless Steel Bar Polishing
Shatrunjay Special Alloy can polish nearly any stainless steel bar item to a #4 polish, #6 Polish, #7 polish or a #8 mirror finish. Stainless steel polished bar can also be cut to size. Pitt free surfaces can also be provided.
2] Heatreating & Annealing
Some grades of stainless steel bars can be heatreated to change the mechanical properties. Precipitation Hardening Stainless Steels such as 17-4PH can be heatreated. 410 stainless steel bar can also be heatreated. In some cases, secondary annealing 300 series stainless steel may be required depending on the application.
3] Positive Material Identification (PMI)
Shatrunjay Special Alloy can perform PMI testing in house for most stainless steel grades, or we can work with independent labs to complete PMI in order to meet customer specifications.
4] UT Testing
In some cases, UT testing of the stainless steel bar may be required. We can assist you with this requirement.
