Stainless Steel Coils

Shatrunjay Special Alloy stocks a wide range of stainless steel coils and sheets in both large and small quantities according to ASTM standards. If you are looking for special sizes of stainless steel slitting coils in India and Export purpose Shatrunjay Special Alloy is the provider with the relationship with domestic and international Mills. We can supply your organisation with competitive price, special sizes of stainless steel coils and slitting coils with the best of Quality and Mill test certificates in Ready stock delivery.

Shatrunjay Special Alloy Export and Supply capabilities include:
  • Thickness 0.25mm to 4.00mm
  • Hardness: - Minimum 3.2mm to maximum 2000mm
  • Finish: - 2B, 2D, Bright Annealed, #3 Polish, #4 Polish and more
  • Width:- soft,half hard, Half Hard, Half Hard, ¾ Hard, Full Hard & Spring Hard
Stainless Steel Coil Grades Available:
  • Stainless Steel coils available in following grades : 304, 304L, 304H, 309S, 309H, 310S, 310H, 316, 316H, 316L, 316TI, 317, 317L, 321, 321H, 347, 347H, 409, 410, 410S, 430, 202, 201
  • Nickel Alloy coils available in following grades :such as Monel, Nickel, Inconel, Hastalloy, Copper, Aluminium, Titanium, Tantalum, Bismuth.
  • Duplex & Super Duplex coils available in following grades : S31803, S32750, S32760, S31500, S32205.
